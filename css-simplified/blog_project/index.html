Doom 3 Prologue

February 24th, 2140

The prologue to *Doom 3* sets the stage for the game's terrifying narrative. Set in the year 2145, the Union Aerospace Corporation (UAC) has established a research facility on Mars. There, they uncover an ancient alien artifact, which unbeknownst to them, opens a portal to Hell itself. Demonic forces begin to spill out, wreaking havoc on the facility, and the player takes on the role of a space marine sent to investigate the mysterious and deadly occurrences.

As the protagonist, you explore the decaying UAC facility, facing horrors from beyond as the world around you collapses into chaos. The haunting atmosphere, paired with a series of unsettling events, reveals that the company's greed and thirst for power have led to a catastrophic downfall. As the lead programmer, Carmack was responsible for pushing the technical boundaries of gaming at the time. He was particularly known for his work on the game's advanced graphics engine, which utilized real-time lighting and shadow techniques that were groundbreaking for the era.
